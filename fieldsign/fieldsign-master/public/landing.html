How it works

From job creation to
signed report in 3 steps

No training required. Technicians are up and running in under 5 minutes.

01
Admin creates SWO & prints QR
Create a work order, assign to a technician, set the service type. Generate and print the technician's QR code once — use it forever.
02
Technician scans QR on-site
The technician scans their QR with any phone camera. Lands directly on their mobile app — sees assigned jobs, client details, and job instructions.
03
Report submitted, PDF sent instantly
Technician fills the form, gets the client to sign on the screen. PDF is generated and emailed to both client and your office — automatically.
